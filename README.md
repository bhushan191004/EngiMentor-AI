# EngiMentor AI

## SDG 4 – Quality Education

EngiMentor AI is an AI-powered Engineering Learning Assistant developed using Botpress and Generative AI technologies. The project aims to support Sustainable Development Goal 4 (Quality Education) by providing students with instant academic assistance, concept explanations, revision support, exam preparation guidance, and viva preparation resources.

## Problem Statement

Many engineering students struggle to access personalized academic support outside classroom hours. Limited access to teachers, complex study materials, and lack of structured guidance often lead to learning gaps and poor academic performance.

## Solution

EngiMentor AI addresses this challenge by providing an intelligent educational assistant that uses Artificial Intelligence and a subject-specific Knowledge Base to answer student queries.

The chatbot can:

* Explain engineering concepts
* Summarize lengthy topics
* Generate viva questions
* Provide revision notes
* Assist with exam preparation
* Recommend learning resources

## Features

### Concept Explanation

Provides simple and detailed explanations of academic concepts.

### Topic Summarization

Generates concise summaries of lengthy topics for quick revision.

### Viva Preparation

Creates viva questions and answers for interview and oral examination preparation.

### Revision Support

Generates short notes and important points for quick review.

### Exam Guidance

Provides subject-specific preparation strategies and study plans.

### Knowledge Base Integration

Uses uploaded engineering notes to generate context-aware responses.
